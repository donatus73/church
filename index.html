Our Story

Word of Hope Evangelical Mission is a modern-day family rooted in timeless truth. We meet in the heart of the city, gathering people from all backgrounds to experience authentic community and life-changing faith.

Our mission: to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus, to love without limits, and to serve our neighborhoods with compassion. Whether you’re just starting to explore faith or looking for a church home, there’s a place for you here.
